Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2025

1. Introduction

ConnectHealth ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information from TikTok OAuth

When you authenticate with TikTok using our OAuth flow, we may receive the following information from TikTok:

  • Open ID
  • Display Name
  • Avatar URL
  • Basic user information as permitted by the user.info.basic scope

B. Local Storage Data

We store the following data in your browser's localStorage:

  • Access Token (provided by TikTok OAuth)
  • Token Expiration Time
  • Token Creation Timestamp
  • User Display Information (optional)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you with TikTok and maintain your session
  • To enable you to create and manage advertising campaigns
  • To display personalized user information in the application
  • To improve and optimize the Service

4. Data Storage and Security

Your access token and personal information are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data is:

  • Not transmitted to any external servers or databases
  • Not backed up or synchronized across devices
  • Cleared when you log out or clear browser data
  • Only accessible within the ConnectHealth application domain

Important: While we protect data in localStorage, browsers can be compromised. For production applications handling sensitive data, we recommend using secure backend authentication.

5. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with TikTok's OAuth and APIs. Your use of TikTok services is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for TikTok's data practices.

6. Data Retention

Your access token is stored in localStorage until:

  • The token expires (based on TikTok's expiration time)
  • You explicitly log out
  • You clear your browser's localStorage or cookies
  • You clear your browser's browsing data

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Log out at any time to clear your session
  • Request deletion of your OAuth token by logging out
  • Revoke application access through your TikTok account settings
  • Clear your browser's localStorage to remove stored data

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Service constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@connecthealth.example.com
