Privacy Policy

HealthConnect - TikTok Ads Integration

1. Information Collection

This Application collects minimal information necessary for TikTok OAuth authentication:

  • TikTok Access Token: Stored locally in your browser only
  • OAuth State Parameter: Used for CSRF protection, not stored permanently
  • Form Data: Ad campaign details are not sent to external servers in this implementation

2. Data Storage

All tokens and authentication data are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. No personal or authentication data is transmitted to or stored on HealthConnect servers.

3. Third-Party Services

This Application communicates with:

  • TikTok OAuth Server: For authentication
  • TikTok API: For user verification (user.info.basic scope)

Your use of TikTok's services is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

4. Security

⚠️ Important: This frontend-only implementation exchanges OAuth tokens in the browser. In a production environment, token exchange must occur on a secure backend server with proper HTTPS/TLS encryption.

5. User Rights

You can:

  • Clear your localStorage to remove stored tokens at any time
  • Revoke application access through your TikTok account settings
  • Request deletion of any data by clearing your browser data

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

HealthConnect may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the Application indicates acceptance of any changes.

7. Contact & Support

For privacy concerns, contact TikTok's support through their official channels.
