Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

1. Introduction

HealthConnect ("we," "our," or "the app") is a demonstration application created solely for educational and testing purposes to showcase TikTok OAuth authentication integration.

2. TikTok Data Usage

Important Notice: This application uses TikTok solely for authentication purposes.

  • No Content Publishing: We do NOT publish, create, or modify any content on TikTok
  • No Content Access: We do NOT access, read, or download your TikTok videos or posts
  • Authentication Only: We only use TikTok to verify your identity through OAuth login
  • Minimal Data: We only receive basic profile information (username, user ID) required for authentication

3. Data Collection

The only data we collect includes:

  • TikTok User ID (for authentication)
  • Username (for display purposes)
  • Profile picture URL (optional, for display)

We do NOT collect:

  • Your TikTok videos or content
  • Your followers or following list
  • Your likes, comments, or interactions
  • Your location data
  • Any sensitive personal information
  • Any health or fitness data

4. How We Use Your Data

The limited data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating your login session
  • Displaying your username in the app interface
  • Testing and demonstrating OAuth integration

5. Data Storage and Security

  • This is a client-side demo application with no backend server
  • No data is permanently stored on any server
  • Session data exists only in your browser's memory during your visit
  • Data is cleared when you close your browser or log out

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with any third parties. This is a standalone demo application with no external integrations beyond TikTok OAuth.

7. Educational and Testing Purpose

This application is created exclusively for:

  • Educational demonstration of OAuth authentication
  • Testing TikTok login integration
  • App review and approval processes

It is NOT intended for production use or commercial purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to this app at any time through your TikTok account settings
  • Request information about what data we have (minimal profile info only)
  • Request deletion of your data (which is automatically cleared on logout)

9. Children's Privacy

This demo app is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

As this is a demo application, this policy is subject to change for testing purposes. Any updates will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Information

This is a demonstration application. For questions about TikTok's privacy practices, please refer to TikTok's official privacy policy.
